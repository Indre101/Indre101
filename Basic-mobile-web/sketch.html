The sketched logo for folders
I enjoy the aspects of a style that is minimal but effective, and the play with shapes and clean sharp lines. The way the letters would interfere with each other and the interesting angles of characters and I do think if I were to choose shades of deeper reds and oranges to use as a coloring for the the logo would have a resemblence to Saul Bass style.
