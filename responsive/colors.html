An example of Saul Bass movie poster

I decided to stay within the same color scheme as I used in the Project document in order to reflect the Saul Bass style. The background color is called Hint Of Red, it is not a very saturated color compared to all the other colors so I think it works really well in creating saturation contrast and bringing attention to the areas where it is needed.

Orange and red tones together with black and purple tones create a cold and warm colors contrast, which was used a lot in Saul Bass movie posters. In the example poster on the right you can see a very effective use of contrast of extension, the striking red is well balanced with a minimal use of black color in lettering and ilustration.

Posterswere usually with just one distinct bacground color, so S. Bass leaned towards reds and red oranges. He believed that these colors attract attention, and they way he used to create contrasts on posters, with black lettering or figures was a way to also keep his designs very outspoken but minimalistic.- Kasia Pietkut
