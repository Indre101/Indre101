Welcome

This page is dedicated to Saul Bass Organic style, his Typography, color scheme and a bit about Saul Bass himself. Here You will also find other usefull information about the page it self.

Often You can find Saul Bass described as The man who changed graphic design it is quite a bold statement, but after reading and reshearching who is Saul Bass and his impact, there is no doubt about his importance in the graphic design industry.

I can be honest in saying that I knew very little about Saul Bass. But apparently I was always seeing his work and legacy in graphic design. His beginning mostly was working with corporations and developing their logos. Such companies as AT&T, Kleenex or Bell are still using his created work, it just tells how timeless his style still is.
